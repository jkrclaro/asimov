About

Every small business owner knows word of mouth can be a powerful way to grow a business. We help small businesses get more referrals by making it easier for customers to refer a business to their friends by utilizing proven referral marketing strategies and tactics built into the service.

We care deeply about trust

At Rewardplex, we're convinced that every transaction is based on trust. It doesn't matter if you're buying your dream car or just dinner. You only want to do business with someone you believe.

So what does this mean if you're a merchant selling your wares in the proverbial village marketplace? How do you use trust to guide your next customer to you? The key is to get someone who trusts you, to tell others who don't know you, about you.

And this is the essence of referral marketing. Its power lies in the personal relationships that pass trust along. This is what makes referral marketing important and it's clear that it works. We want to make it easy for you to harness trust to grow your business.
